Frequently Asked Questions

Is this app really free?

Yes! It is open-source software. You can use it indefinitely for free.

Do I need internet?

No. The app works 100% offline. Internet is only required if you want to use the optional Gemini AI features.

Where is my data stored?

Locally on your device in your AppData folder. We do not have any servers and cannot see your invoices.
